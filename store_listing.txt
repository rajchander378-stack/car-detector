Ever spotted a car and wondered what it is? AutoSpotter identifies any vehicle in seconds using advanced AI.

HOW IT WORKS
Just point your camera at a car — or pick a photo from your gallery — and AutoSpotter will tell you exactly what it is. Using advanced AI assistance, you get detailed results including:

• Make and model
• Year range and generation
• Trim level and body style
• Colour identification
• Confidence score (0–100%)
• Distinguishing visual features and AI observations

NUMBER PLATE READING & UK VALUATION
AutoSpotter reads visible number plates automatically. For UK-registered vehicles, tap the valuation button to get current market prices including retail, trade, and private sale estimates — all from one photo.

UPLOAD FROM GALLERY
Don't have a car in front of you? Upload any car photo from your gallery or camera roll and get the same full identification and valuation.

SAVE & FAVOURITE
Sign in with your Google account to save your identifications. Bookmark your favourites for quick access later. Your scan history is stored securely and synced across sessions.

SMART IMAGE PROCESSING
AutoSpotter automatically optimises your photos before analysis — adjusting for exposure, resolution, and framing — so you get accurate results even in tricky conditions.

BUILT FOR THE UK
Results use UK English spelling and terminology. Body styles are named the British way — saloon, estate, coupé — not the American way. Valuations use UK vehicle data.

FEATURES AT A GLANCE
✓ Instant AI car identification from camera or gallery
✓ Make, model, year, trim, body style, and colour
✓ Confidence score with visual gauge
✓ Automatic number plate reading
✓ UK vehicle valuation (retail, trade, and private prices)
✓ Save scan history to your account
✓ Favourite and bookmark identifications
✓ Smart image optimisation for better accuracy
✓ Beautiful results display with spec chips and price cards
✓ Google Sign-In for secure, hassle-free accounts

PRIVACY & DATA
AutoSpotter takes your privacy seriously. Photos are processed transiently and are not stored on our servers. Your account data is secured with Firebase.
