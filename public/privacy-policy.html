Axiom Forge Software ("we", "us", "our") operates the AutoSpotter mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you sign in with Google, we receive and store:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Profile photo URL
  • Google user ID

This information is stored securely in Google Cloud Firestore.

Vehicle Images

When you capture a photo for identification, the image is sent to Google Gemini (Google's AI service) for analysis. Images are processed transiently and are not stored on our servers. Google's use of this data is governed by their own privacy policy.

Number Plates

If a UK number plate (Vehicle Registration Mark) is visible in your photo, it may be read by the AI. If you request a price valuation, the registration is sent to a third-party UK vehicle data API to retrieve valuation information. We do not permanently store number plate data.

Usage Data

We may record basic usage information such as scan timestamps and identification results to improve the service.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide AI-powered vehicle identification
  • To retrieve UK vehicle valuations using detected number plates
  • To maintain your user account
  • To investigate and resolve reported issues with AI results

3. Third-Party Services

We share data with the following third-party services to provide the app's functionality:

  • Google Firebase — authentication, data storage, and AI processing (Gemini)
  • UK Vehicle Data API — vehicle valuation lookups using registration numbers

Each third-party service is governed by its own privacy policy. We only share the minimum data necessary for each service to function.

4. Data Retention

Your account data is retained for as long as your account is active. Vehicle images are not stored beyond the immediate processing session. You may delete your account and all associated data at any time (see section 7).

5. Data Security

All data transmitted between the app and our servers is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS. Data stored in Firestore is encrypted at rest by Google Cloud.

6. Your Rights (UK GDPR / Data Protection Act 2018)

Under UK data protection law, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your data (see section 7)
  • Portability — request your data in a portable format
  • Object — object to processing of your data
  • Restrict — request restriction of processing

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email address below.

7. Account and Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time:

  • In the app: Go to Settings > Delete Account
  • Via the web: Visit our account deletion page

Deletion removes your profile, scan history, AI reports, and all associated data from our systems. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

8. Children's Privacy

AutoSpotter is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by updating the effective date at the top of this page.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your data rights, please contact us:

Axiom Forge Software

Email: privacy@axiomforgesoftware.com

11. Lawful Basis for Processing

We process your personal data on the following lawful bases under UK GDPR:

  • Consent — you consent to data processing when you sign in and accept our terms
  • Contract — processing is necessary to provide the service you requested
  • Legitimate interest — processing AI reports and improving the service
