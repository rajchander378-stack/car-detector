Image Requirements

  • Each image must contain exactly one vehicle as the primary subject.
  • Must be a real photograph — not a drawing, sketch, 3D render, toy, or model car.
  • The vehicle must be clearly visible — not too far away, heavily obscured, or only partially visible.
  • Must not be a screenshot from a website, video game, or another app.
  • JPEG or PNG format only.
  • Minimum resolution: 200x200 pixels.
  • Avoid extremely dark, bright, or blurry images.
  • Number plates should be legible where possible — this enables automatic valuation lookups.
